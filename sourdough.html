You gotta love sourdough. here's a picture of my favourite country loaf from Tartine Bakery!

Description

  • One fabulous loaf of bread. Crust is great. Maybe we will do our hummus recipe next? Let me know what you'd like to see down in the comments below.

Ingredients

  • Starter
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Good vibes

Steps

  1. Grab the Starter
  2. Add water and mix it all up
  3. let rest
  4. add flour and salt
  5. let rest again
  6. more resting
  7. preheat oven and cast iron super hot at 500 F*
  8. 30 minutes lid on, 30 minutes lid off
  9. let rest before cutting into it
